Our community is based on good will and trust.

Make friends, have fun and enjoy variety of services and events

Here you can learn and teach for free!

Offer

By pushing 'create offer' button on the navbar you can start creating an offer

You should create offers whenever you feel like sharing one of your skills with some people

Offers are divided into 2 types, 'Service' and 'Event'

By offering a service, you should teach something to people. That is why this type of offer requires credit to join for other users

Each hour of teaching is worth one credit, which gets calculated by our server

People can send a request to join your offer and you can either accept or decline them. But bear in mind that you only have time to do so, till the application deadline

After accepting people, you should send them a message by going to theri profile and pushing 'Send message' button, containing the specific location of your offer, as users usually share the district of offer only

People that had been accepted to your offer, can cancel their application till the cancelation deadline, so that they can save their credits.

Take into account that your participants can leave your offer anytime before start time, they will just lose their promised credits to the server

When your service is finished, both you and your participant need to confirm for credits to transfer

Credits wont transfer if only your participants dont confirm taking your service.

Your participants have 3 days to confirm, in case they ignore it, the server would automatically confirm and send the promised credits to you

Another type of offer is event, where it is not necessarily about teaching, you usually create it for people to have fun

Note: After reaching 15 credits, you can no longer offer, so you better start taking services

Default offers are displayed based on your location, but you can filter them based on a new location, interests, date and etc

Please feel responsible for our community and push the 'Report' button in case you see any inappropriate offer

Rate

When both you and your offer participant confirm taking the offer

Then you can go to your participant's profile and click on the 'Rate & Review' button

There you can both rate your participants and leave a comment, based on their behavior

Bear in mind that your participants can rate you, as well as leaving a review on your profile

These rates and reviews are one of the factors for people to be either willing or hesitant in accepting you for their offers or sending a request to join your offer

Please feel responsible for our community and push the 'Report' button in case you see any inappropriate comments

Share

Just take some time, and improve your profile

Since other users usually send friend requests to those that share the same location, interests and skills

When you accept a friend request, both of you will become each others friends automatically

You can send your friends a message by going to their profiles and keep track of their activities

Please feel responsible for our community and push the 'Report' button in case you see any inappropriate profile
